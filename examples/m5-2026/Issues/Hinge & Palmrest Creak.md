# Hinge & Palmrest Creak

A widely-discussed Reddit report described creaking from the hinge and palmrest area on a $2,000 M5 MBP, with Apple Store employees reportedly dismissing the complaint. Echoes hinge issues on prior MBP generations.

## Symptoms

- Audible creak when opening / closing the lid.
- Creak when applying pressure to the palmrest area, or twisting the chassis slightly.
- May intensify over time, early signs are worth catching.

## How to detect on-site

Pure manual inspection, easy and fast:

1. Open and close the lid slowly several times. Listen.
2. Open lid to a few different angles (45°, 90°, 110°). Listen for movement.
3. Press firmly on each corner of the palmrest with the lid open. Listen / feel.
4. Gently twist the chassis (one hand on each side of the keyboard). Should feel solid.
5. Tap around the trackpad and surrounding area.

A healthy unibody MBP should feel like one piece of metal. No audible response to flex, no give in the palmrest.

## Likely fix (after purchase, not a verification thing)

Tightening hinge screws often resolves creak on prior generations, but you don't want to be doing this on a brand-new unit. **If it creaks in store, ask for a different unit.**
